Description
-
3D models and images of dendrites in neurons for data analysis.
Data acquisition
-

Samples from the visual brain cortex are first collected from a neurodegeneration model (mouse) and prepared for imaging.
3D Modelling
-

Images are used to make 3D models in IMARIS, a modelling software. Afterward data is extracted using this program for further analysis.
Data Analysis
-

MATLAB is used to extract information based on the 3D model such as density of dendritic spines on a dendrite, the dimensions of spine heads and more. For more information, see congress presentation below.
